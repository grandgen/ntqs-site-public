Services

Services offered by NTQS

Quality System development and Remediation

We offer curated quality system development master plan from supporting early stage of design realization through marketing/commercialization to post market surveillance for class I to class III medical devices regardless of the size of the company. Our regulatory expertise include, implantable, diagnostics, wearables, SaMDs (software as a medical device). We offer help in either over all creation/implementation of whole customized QMS or just specific areas QMS as per need basis- Design Control, Risk Management, Internal/Supplier audits, CAPA, Complaint handling, Vendor approval list, Verification /validation plans and reports.

Quality Training

Definition of quality system includes not only processes and procedure but also the organizational structure, responsibilities and resources necessary to manage quality. Quality management training to all the employees as per their roles and responsibility is crucial for a compliance and success of the company. The areas of training includes- Quality Manual, Quality Policy, Complaint handling and to any process or procedure that is applicable to employees functional area. We can design training to our client to suit their needs with engaging presentations to include quality system regulations and the attestation to the training for audit trail. Some of the commonly used training programs are for CAPA, Complaint handling, Design control, Validation/verification studies.

Regulatory Support

Nobel Touch quality systems provide regulatory support throughout all the stages of product life cycle to be compliant with the regulations of medical device markets including United States, Canada, Brazil, Europe, Australia, and Japan. Every country has their own sets of regulations to follow and are monitored by notified bodies. Our experts understand those regulations and work with each client to ensure that the quality system is compliant with the regulations of intended market. Either you are looking for Compliance audits and assessment, Third party certification, Quality system development and remediation, 483/warning letter/ resolutions and responses or complaint handling for your business, Nobel Touch is one stop shop.
