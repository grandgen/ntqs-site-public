Services offered by NTQS

Internal audit/Supplier audit

Identifying flaws in your own quality management system preferred approach than caught by FDA or regulatory authorities which can even result in revoked license to market medical devices.

Our team of ASQ certified medical device auditors demonstrate expertise in auditing all the functional elements of the QMS at once or in parts (Major subsystem/ Minor Subsystem) as needed based on CFR820, ISO13485, EU MDR regulations. We practice risk-based approach to identify risks and opportunities for our clints.

Medical device design control

In a medical device regulatory world, device shall be manufactured such that during normal condition of use are suitable for the intended purpose, safe, and effective. The risk should be reduced as far as possible. We provide support in each aspect of the design and manufacturing process from product realization through verification/ validation studies to post market planning and process. Our experts will help you to develop Risk management process, Clinical evaluation process, Technical documentation at each step of design and development, corrective action and recall process to name a few.

Medical device Validation

Product and process validation, throughout the lifecycle of medical devices from development/manufacturing to post market is a crucial quality management system requirement. We provide services for setting qualifications, validation/verification activities for product manufacturing process, facility and equipment validation studies, Test method validation studies, Master validation plan, software tool validation, SaMD validation. Our services includes writing customized templates for test protocols and reports, clinical trials plan and reports, Supporting IQ/PQ/OQ and other documentation that ensure the reliability and effectiveness of the device with its intended use, supporting you through facility/process improvements or Gap analysis.
